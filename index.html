About Us

Who We Are

Switch Digital Production Enterprise is a registered enterprise committed to developing creative industries through event production, digital media, marketing, branding, training, talent development, and strategic partnerships.

Our mission is to create platforms that empower individuals and businesses while contributing to economic development through innovation and entrepreneurship.

We collaborate with government institutions, private organizations, NGOs, educational institutions, hospitality brands, and development partners to execute impactful projects.

What is Face of a Chef?

Face of a Chef is a national culinary competition and food exhibition designed to discover, promote, and empower talented chefs and food entrepreneurs. Our objective is to bridge the gap between culinary talent and commercial opportunities.

Vision

To become Africa’s leading platform for discovering culinary talents, promoting food innovation, and empowering entrepreneurs.

Mission

To create sustainable opportunities for chefs, food businesses, and hospitality professionals through education, competitions, exhibitions, strategic partnerships, and investment.
