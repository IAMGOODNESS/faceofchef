Official Competition Rules & Kitchen Requirements

To maintain the highest standards of culinary excellence and fairness, the Face of a Chef judging committee has released the official rulebook for this year's tournament. All participants must strictly adhere to these guidelines during the live cooking phases.

Judging Criteria

Our panel of expert judges will be scoring each dish based on a 100-point system. The breakdown is as follows:

  • Taste & Flavor Profile (40 pts): The harmony of ingredients, seasoning, and overall deliciousness.
  • Presentation & Plating (30 pts): Visual appeal, creativity, and modern aesthetic techniques.
  • Originality & Concept (15 pts): How well the dish responds to the category brief (e.g., Street Food Innovation).
  • Hygiene & Workflow (15 pts): Cleanliness of the workstation, time management, and professional kitchen etiquette.
"A beautiful plate will get our attention, but an unorganized station or a lack of flavor will cost you the title. Discipline in the kitchen is mandatory."

Time Limits & Ingredient Rules

Competitors will have exactly 60 minutes to complete their dishes from start to finish. A shared pantry will be provided for basic staples, but contestants must bring their own specialized, pre-approved ingredients for their signature dishes. Pre-chopped or pre-cooked items are strictly prohibited unless authorized for specific baking categories.

A comprehensive PDF of the rulebook will be emailed to all successfully registered candidates. We strongly advise you to read it thoroughly before entering the arena.
